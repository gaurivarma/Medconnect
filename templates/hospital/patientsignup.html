Guidelines and Eligibility Criteria

Eligibility: To be eligible for registration as a patient, you must meet the following criteria:

  • You must be a resident of the country in which you are registering.
  • Only individuals with genuine medical needs are eligible.
  • You must submit the required documents including income proof, medical history, and prescription.

Important Notes:

  • Your registration will be considered incomplete if any required document is missing.
  • The hospital reserves the right to verify the information provided during registration.
  • Ensure all personal information is correct and up to date.

By clicking "Register", you agree to comply with all hospital terms and conditions.
